Meet the Designers

Originally Fine Art graduates, Babsy founders Annie Boman and Nicole Smith trained as Goldsmiths before launching their label in 2018. Their goal is to bring contemporary art to life using ethically sourced materials to create unique jewelry that speaks to the Berlin zeitgeist.

The Studio

The Babsy jewelry label is based in and inspired by Berlin. Each piece is conceptualised and crafted by hand, from start to finish, in the 120m2 Kreuzberg studio, directly opposite The Kreuzberg Pavillion.
